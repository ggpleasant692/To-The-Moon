What is a Music Genre?

A music genre is like a distinct flavor or style of music. It's a way of categorizing music based on shared characteristics like rhythm, melody, instrumentation, and cultural influences. Each genre has its own unique sound and vibe, and it often reflects the cultural background and artistic expression of the musicians who create it. From the catchy beats of pop to the raw energy of rock and the intricate melodies of classical, music genres help us navigate the vast and diverse world of music, allowing us to find the sounds that resonate with us the most.
